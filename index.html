BRIEF NIO HISTROY

The organisation started as an idea from a person who does not wish his identity being disclosed he then took the idea to a mosque called Masjid Fatma where he used to attend for prayers it was then agreed between the men that each would bring a sum of around three hundred shilligs monthly at that time, for Idd support for the orphans.

The group has grown into an organisation which has now incorporated paying of school fees that including; ECD school fee, primary school fee, secondary school fee, Madrassa fee and the initial agenda i.e idd support

The organisation is looking for ways to becoming self sustained and thus increase its agendas on supporting the orphan child and increase its support by expanding and creating branches to bring on board more orphans especially those who really need the support- the needy ones- this being said does not mean that there is any orphan going by the criteria of the organisation that is on the locality that the company is currently operating that is denied the chance.
